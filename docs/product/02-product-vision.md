# Product Vision

## Product Vision Statement

AI Knowledge Workspace helps learners quickly recover the exact segment of a previously consumed lecture that contains the knowledge they need.

## Target User

The initial target user is a self-directed learner, such as a university student or early-career learner, who studies from long-form lecture videos and needs a faster way to revisit specific explanations.

## Product Value Proposition

The product provides search-first access to previously processed learning media. Instead of rewatching an entire lecture, the user can search transcript chunks, find the most relevant segment, and review the supporting transcript in context.

## Design Principles

- Search-first recovery over assistant-style interaction
- Results should lead the user back to a relevant media segment, not just a block of transcript text
- Workspace scope should keep assets and retrieval bounded to one user's logical container
- The first release should stay narrow, reliable, and realistic

## What This Product Is Not

- It is not a generic chatbot project.
- It is not a RAG answer-generation product in phase 1.
- It is not a collaboration workspace or team knowledge hub in phase 1.
- It is not a broad multimodal learning platform in the initial release.
